# 🛍️ Retail Business Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a **Retail Business Performance Dashboard** built using **Power BI**, designed to provide actionable insights into sales, profitability, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly monitor key business metrics, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions across products, regions, and customer segments.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to:

* Track overall **business performance**
* Identify **top-performing products and customers**
* Analyze **revenue and profit trends**
* Understand **customer purchasing behavior**
* Monitor **order fulfillment status**

## 📈 Dashboard Insights & Analysis

### 1️⃣ Revenue & Profit Trends

* Monthly trend analysis shows fluctuations in revenue and profit.
* Peak performance observed during mid-year months.
* A declining trend towards the end of the year indicates potential seasonality or demand drop.

---

### 2️⃣ Top Revenue Generating Products

* **Power Bank** is the highest revenue contributor (~2.04M).
* Followed by:

  * Laptop Sleeve
  * Non-stick Pan
  * Tea Set
  * Perfume

📌 Insight: Electronics and utility products dominate revenue contribution.

---

### 3️⃣ Top Profit Generating Products

* Power Bank also leads in profitability (~0.85M).
* Other profitable products include:

  * Non-stick Pan
  * Notebook Pack
  * Shawl (APP 14 & APP 43)

📌 Insight: High revenue ≠ high profit always, but here Power Bank performs well in both.

---

### 4️⃣ Payment Method Analysis

Revenue distribution across payment modes:

* Debit Card (~21%)
* Credit Card (~20%)
* Net Banking (~20%)
* UPI (~19%)
* Cash on Delivery (~19%)

📌 Insight: Digital payments dominate, but COD still holds significant share.

---

### 5️⃣ Order Status Breakdown

* ✅ Delivered: **85.22%**
* ⏳ Pending
* 🔄 Returned
* ❌ Cancelled

📌 Insight: High delivery success rate indicates efficient logistics, but returns/cancellations should be monitored.

---

### 6️⃣ Regional Sales Performance

Top contributing states:

* Gujarat (17.68M)
* Uttar Pradesh (17.28M)
* Maharashtra (15.58M)

Top cities:

* Ahmedabad
* Surat

📌 Insight: Western and Northern regions are key revenue drivers.

---

### 7️⃣ Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

Top customers:

* Sneha Kapoor (~1.29M)
* Rohit Malhotra (~1.25M)
* Kavya Singh (~1.10M)

📌 Insight: A small group of customers contributes significantly to revenue → opportunity for loyalty programs.

## ❓ Business Questions Answered

This dashboard helps answer critical business questions such as:

1. Which products generate the highest revenue and profit?
2. How does revenue and profit trend over time?
3. Which regions (state/city) contribute most to sales?
4. What are the most preferred payment methods?
5. What is the order fulfillment success rate?
6. Who are the most valuable customers?
7. Are there seasonal patterns in sales performance?
8. Which products should be prioritized for growth or optimization?
